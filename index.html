Why Choose WSU Next Carson Coug?

Washington State University (WSU) is renowned for its commitment to academic excellence, vibrant community, and innovative approaches to education. The Carson College of Business at WSU is at the forefront of providing quality education that equips students with the tools they need to thrive in a dynamic and evolving business environment. As part of our ongoing mission to enhance student experiences and outcomes, we are excited to introduce the Next Carson Coug Initiative—a bold step towards redefining business education for future leaders.

The Next Carson Coug Initiative is designed to empower both high school students and post-military individuals by offering a supportive and flexible learning environment that emphasizes real-world skills and career readiness. We are committed to making higher education accessible and impactful through tailored programs that meet the diverse needs of our student population. Our partnership with the Western Undergraduate Exchange (WUE) allows us to offer significant tuition savings for out-of-state students, making a world-class education within reach for more aspiring students.

At the Carson College of Business, we understand the importance of networking and mentorship in a student's career journey. That’s why we leverage the strength of our “Cougs Helping Cougs” alumni network to provide continuous mentorship, career opportunities, and guidance. Our alumni are dedicated to helping the next generation of Cougs succeed by providing connections to job opportunities and supporting students as they transition from academia to the professional world.

The Next Carson Coug Initiative is focused on fostering a learning environment that prioritizes hands-on experience and real-world application of skills. Our programs are designed with a blend of traditional coursework and experiential learning, ensuring students gain valuable practical skills through internships, collaborative projects, and industry partnerships. Whether you are a recent high school graduate looking to start your college journey or a post-military individual seeking to transition into a business career, our programs provide personalized support and career development services to help you succeed.

By choosing WSU and the Carson College of Business, you are choosing an institution that values innovation, community, and success. We are committed to equipping our students with the skills, knowledge, and support necessary to excel in the competitive world of business. Join us and become part of a community that is shaping the future—one that believes in lifting each other up and creating opportunities for all. Together, we can help you achieve your goals and make your mark in the world.
