About Us

The Carson College of Business at Washington State University has a long-standing tradition of academic excellence, preparing students to become tomorrow's business leaders. Our programs emphasize practical, hands-on learning, which allows students to gain invaluable skills that are directly applicable in the workforce. Our goal is to provide a nurturing and challenging environment that supports student growth, both academically and professionally.

As part of the Next Carson Coug Initiative, we are focusing on expanding accessibility to business education for high school students and post-military individuals. Our faculty is dedicated to providing personalized mentorship, fostering a culture of innovation, and helping students develop the skills they need to make an impact in their communities and beyond.
