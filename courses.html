The Next Carson Coug Initiative

The Next Carson Coug Initiative is designed to position Washington State University's Carson College of Business as the first choice for business education in the Pacific Northwest. Our mission is to empower future Cougs with the knowledge, skills, and networks needed to excel in their careers. This initiative is focused on creating an inclusive, supportive environment for high school students and post-military individuals, ensuring that graduates are career-ready and can hit the ground running.

Program Goals

  • Increase WUE (Western Undergraduate Exchange) enrollment by 2% in 18 months.
  • Focus on marketing through a social media campaign targeting graduating high school students in WUE states.
  • Highlight key benefits such as WUE tuition savings, strong career outcomes, and the "Cougs Helping Cougs" alumni network.

Key Strengths of WSU and the Next Carson Coug Initiative

  • Strong Academic Programs: WSU offers a wide range of well-regarded academic programs, including business, engineering, agriculture, and more.
  • Research Opportunities: As an R1 research-intensive university, WSU provides numerous opportunities for students to engage in meaningful research.
  • Powerful Alumni Network: The "Cougs Helping Cougs" alumni network is instrumental in providing mentorship, career guidance, and support to students.

Marketing Strategy

The marketing strategy for the Next Carson Coug Initiative revolves around leveraging social media to engage prospective students and their parents. We will focus on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and YouTube to showcase the benefits of joining the Carson College of Business.

  • Instagram and TikTok: Focused on visual engagement and student testimonials, showcasing real student experiences and career outcomes.
  • LinkedIn and Facebook: Targeted towards parents and guardians, highlighting affordability, career success, and the "Cougs Helping Cougs" support network.
  • YouTube: Virtual tours, alumni interviews, and videos highlighting the benefits of WUE tuition savings and career readiness.

Implementation Timeline

The implementation of the Next Carson Coug Initiative will take place over six months, with specific goals for each month:

  • Month 1: Planning and initial setup, including strategy alignment and content creation using AI tools.
  • Month 2: Campaign launch with testimonials on Instagram and TikTok, focusing on WUE students and career outcomes.
  • Month 3: Expand campaigns with user-generated content (UGC) to encourage students to share their WSU stories.
  • Month 4: Mid-campaign review to assess performance and refine strategies.
  • Month 5: Boost parental involvement through targeted Facebook campaigns and scale UGC efforts.
  • Month 6: Final push for enrollment with ads emphasizing WUE savings and career success. Generate a final report on campaign performance.

Key Performance Indicators

The success of the Next Carson Coug Initiative will be measured through the following key performance indicators:

  • 2% increase in WUE enrollment (35,400 total students).
  • 40% boost in social media engagement.
  • 10% increase in application completions.
  • 15% rise in interest in the "Cougs Helping Cougs" career services.
